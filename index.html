Skittles Candy

Skittles are a brand of fruit-flavored candy, currently produced and marketed by the Wrigley Company, a division of Mars, Inc. Skittles consist of hard sugar shells imprinted with the letter "S" and filled with fruit juice, flavorings, and sugar. The original flavors were orange, lemon, lime, grape, and strawberry. The current flavors are orange, lemon, lime, grape, strawberry, watermelon, raspberry, pineapple, passion fruit, mango, and blue raspberry. The original flavors were introduced in the United States in 1974, and the current flavors were introduced in 2006. The candies are sold in a variety of sizes, including single-serve bags, sharing bags, and multi-serve boxes.
